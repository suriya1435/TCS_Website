About Us

The Chennai Silks is fashioned by unlimited possibilities! Since its establishment in 2006, the brand has evolved into a trusted name known for excellence, authenticity, and innovation. What began as a passion for textiles has now grown into a diverse business group with successful ventures in Textiles, Jewelry, Retailing, Education, and Green Energy. With a dedicated workforce of over 3,000 employees, we continue to expand our footprint while upholding strong values, ethical practices, and a customer-first approach. At The Chennai Silks, we take pride in offering products and experiences that blend tradition with modernity, ensuring quality that stands the test of time. Our journey reflects not only growth in scale but progress in purpose — creating opportunities, supporting craftsmanship, and contributing to a sustainable future. As we continue to evolve, we remain committed to delivering excellence and enriching every life we touch, today and for generations ahead.

Our Founder

The Madras Silks was founded in 1962 and has since become a cherished name synonymous with timeless elegance, exceptional craftsmanship, innovative designs, and unwavering customer trust. As a proud group company of The Chennai Silks, The Madras Silks upholds the visionary values laid down by our founder, Shri A. Kulandaivel Mudaliar.

Guided by his inspiring vision, what began as a humble weaving unit has grown into a diversified textile and retail enterprise, redefining excellence in the world of silk and traditional fashion. Decades of dedication and perfection have enabled the brand to leave an enduring imprint on the Indian textile industry, offering an exquisite range of silk sarees, traditional attire, and contemporary designs crafted with precision and passion.

Today, The Madras Silks continues its legacy by embracing modern retail practices, ethical business standards, and a strong digital presence. Rooted in tradition yet driven by innovation, we remain committed to delivering quality, transparency, and timeless beauty, ensuring that every creation becomes a part of cherished moments for generations to come.
