About Us

The Chennai Silks is fashioned by unlimited possibilities! Founded in 2006, the group has grown into multiple commercial sectors including Textiles, Jewelry, Retailing, Education, and Green Energy. With a workforce of over 3000 employees, the brand continues to expand with strong values and innovation.

THIRU. K. VINAYAKAM
FOUNDER CHAIRMAN – The Chennai Silks

Mr. K. Vinayakam transformed a modest textile business into a strong business conglomerate. Under his leadership, The Chennai Silks and Sree Kumaran Thangamaligai have become iconic brands.

THIRU. V. VIKRAM NARAYEN
EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR – The Chennai Silks

Mr. Vikram Narayen continues the family tradition of quality and innovation. With strong business acumen, he leads brand partnerships and strategic growth.
