The Chennai Silks

Founded in 1954 by the visionary Kulandavel Mudaliar, The Chennai Silks began as a humble yarn store rooted in passion, dedication, and trust. Over the decades, that small beginning has transformed into Tamil Nadu’s largest and most respected textile chain, known for exceptional craftsmanship, innovation, and customer care. With a strong presence across multiple cities, The Chennai Silks continues to preserve the beauty of traditional weaving while embracing modern designs and technologies. Guided by heritage and driven by excellence, we remain committed to offering quality fabrics, timeless collections, and memorable shopping experiences that inspire generations. Today, The Chennai Silks stands not only as a brand — but as a legacy woven with pride, culture, and progress.

Sree Kumaran Thangamaligai

Sree Kumar Thangamaaligai is a name synonymous with purity, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance in the world of fine jewelry. Built on a foundation of trust and tradition, the brand has earned the loyalty of families across generations by offering meticulously crafted ornaments that reflect cultural heritage and refined beauty. Each masterpiece is designed with precision, blending traditional artistry with contemporary styling to suit every celebration, occasion, and personal milestone. With a strong commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction, Sree Kumar Thangamaaligai continues to be a trusted destination for those seeking jewelry that is not only exquisite, but meaningful — a treasure to cherish today and pass on tomorrow.
